About

I'm a Computer Science student currently in my final year of undergraduate studies at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I've dived into a Design Thinking course during the Summer of 2016 and made a simple exercise game made for the elderly. Currently, I'm working on my final year project: VR Game with Unity and Leap Motion.

My aim is to hone my skills as a programmer by taking part in an internship or a graduate job.

I've a wide variety of interests, spanning from sports to foreign language, performing arts to video games.

I was a long jumper for HKUST's Track and Field varsity team, but more importantly I'm also an athlete who enjoys playing a variety of sports and likes to challenge myself. I'm at near-intermediate level with Japanese and just started on learning German this year.

I have played drum for the past 12 years, though my skills now are rusty due to the limited conditions in my university life. Of course at heart I'm a gamer, be it PC, mobile or console games, I play them all.
